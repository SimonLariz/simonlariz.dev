About Me

Hello there! I'm Simon Lariz, a computer science student at Cal State Fullerton. I am currently pursuing my bachelor's degree in Computer Science with a strong commitment to academic excellence.

As a student, my days revolve around the exciting world of coding, debugging, and crafting projects that fuel my passion for technology. You can explore a plethora of my tech ventures under the "Projects" tab and on my GitHub page. I have an unyielding commitment to continuous learning, and I find joy in breaking things to gain a deeper understanding of their inner workings.

Outside the realm of programming, you might catch me at the gym, lifting and placing things down, channeling my energy into fitness. Alternatively, I immerse myself in various sounds ( currently in my shoegaze phase ). Chess serves as my cerebral escape, where I embrace both strategizing and the humbling recognition of human fallibility.

Drawing from my experience in Python and C++, I consistently endeavor to refine my skills and expand my expertise in the realm of programming. Additionally, I am currently delving into web development and contemplating the exploration of the Rust programming language.

Beyond my technical pursuits, my ultimate vision is to utilize my skills to uplift others and make a positive impact in their lives. Life is a wild ride, and I'm driven to seize every moment to its fullest potential.

And, yes, I enjoy a good laugh! Humor is an essential ingredient in my journey, and I believe in finding joy and amusement in every situation. Laughter lights up the path forward, making the adventure all the more worthwhile.

This website was inspired by the golden era of the 90s and early 00s. I wanted to create something that drew inspiration from the vibrant and colorful websites of the past, delving into the captivating nostalgia of the early internet - from the neon colors and funky fonts to the animated gifs and tiled backgrounds.

Moreover, I felt compelled to break away from the cookie-cutter mold of modern personal portfolio websites that often resemble more of a billboard than a genuine representation of one's creativity and individuality. Here, I strive to showcase my work and passions in a way that resonates with my personality, rejecting the conformity and embracing the quirky charm that sets me apart. Through this digital playground, I hope to evoke a sense of authenticity and engage visitors with a unique experience that is both a nod to the past and a celebration of the boundless possibilities of the present.

Check the links in the sidebar for more resources and possibly a laugh or two.
